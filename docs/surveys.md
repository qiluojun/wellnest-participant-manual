# Surveys

WellNest includes a Pre-Study Survey, daily surveys, and a Weekly Survey. The app will notify you when daily surveys are available.

> Important: Survey buttons are available only during their scheduled time windows. If a button is disabled, the questionnaire is currently closed.

## Pre-Study Survey

After permissions are complete, tap **Join Study** in WellNest.

![Screenshot: Join Study button]({{ '/assets/images/join-study-toggle.png' | relative_url }})

You will then be guided to the **Pre-Study Survey**. Please take your time and answer each question carefully. Your responses help the research team interpret the data collected during the study.

![Screenshot: Pre-Study Survey card]({{ '/assets/images/pre-study-survey-card.png' | relative_url }})

## Pilot Phase

Before formal data collection begins, you will go through a pilot phase of approximately one week.

The tasks during the pilot phase are the same as the tasks during the formal study. This week is a chance to become comfortable with the app and the daily survey routine.

## Daily Surveys

Please complete two short surveys each day: one in the morning and one in the evening.

| Survey | When | What it asks about | Notification |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Morning Survey | 6:00-10:00 am | Previous night's sleep | Yes |
| Evening Survey | 8:00 pm-12:00 am | Daytime and evening experiences before sleep | Yes |

The app sends a notification when a survey becomes available.

![Screenshot: Daily survey card]({{ '/assets/images/daily-survey-card.png' | relative_url }})

![Screenshot: Survey reminder in notification shade]({{ '/assets/images/survey-reminder-notification-shade.png' | relative_url }})

![Screenshot: Evening Survey notification banner]({{ '/assets/images/evening-survey-notification-banner.png' | relative_url }})

If a survey button says **Not completed** and is active, you can tap it to answer the survey. If the button is disabled, the questionnaire is currently closed.

## Morning Survey

The Morning Survey asks about your previous night's sleep.

Please complete it between **6:00-10:00 am**.

## Evening Survey

The Evening Survey asks about your daytime and evening experiences before sleep.

Please complete it between **8:00 pm-12:00 am**.

## Weekly Survey

Please complete the **Weekly Survey** once a week, on Mondays.

The Weekly Survey button appears on the main screen on Monday.

![Screenshot: Weekly Survey card]({{ '/assets/images/weekly-survey-card.png' | relative_url }})

If you cannot complete it on Monday, you may access it later from the **Help screen** by tapping **Weekly Survey**.

![Screenshot: Weekly Survey button on Help screen]({{ '/assets/images/help-screen-weekly-survey.png' | relative_url }})

## Messages from the research team

You may receive messages from the research team in WellNest.

![Screenshot: Messages from Admin area]({{ '/assets/images/messages-from-admin.png' | relative_url }})

Please check messages if the research team contacts you through the app.
